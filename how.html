How to Use

Want to keep your schedule handy? Add this site to your phone.

Privacy

Your favorite events are saved **locally to your phone**. To make sure you don't lose your list when you close your browser, follow the steps below to "install" it first!

iPhone users

  • 1 Tap the Share button (the little square with an arrow).
  • 1 Click on More... button.
  • 2 Find Add to Home Screen in the list.
  • 3 Hit Add and you're good to go!

Android users

  • 1 Tap the three dots in the corner.
  • 2 Pick Install app or Add to Home screen.
  • 3 Confirm it, and check your app drawer!

We don't track you. No details are shared or stored anywhere outside your own phone. Made with love from Salem, OR.

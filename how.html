How to Use

I built this to help me manage my Valentango events. For best results, add this as an site to your phone's homescreen.

Privacy

Your favorite events are saved locally to your phone. To make sure you don't lose your list when you close your browser, follow the steps below to "install" it first!

iPhone users

  • 1 Tap the ... button next to where you enter a web address.
  • 2 Tap the Share button (the little square with an arrow).
  • 3 Click on More (...) button.
  • 4 Find Add to Home Screen in the list.
  • 5 Hit Add and you're good to go!

Android users

  • 1 Tap the three dots in the corner.
  • 2 Pick Install app or Add to Home screen.
  • 3 Confirm it, and check your app drawer!

We don't track you. No details are shared or stored anywhere outside your own phone. I don't want your data. Made with love from Salem, OR.

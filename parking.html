Parking

Overnight Guests

Overnight guests have complementary parking using their guestroom keycard.

Non-Overnight Guests

Non-overnight guests of the festival also have complimentary parking by using the following procedure:

  • 1 As you drive into the parking lot, pick up a white parking ticket and bring with you into the hotel.
  • 2 Inside the hotel, pick up a green parking pass from the hotel registration desk or the ValenTango registration table.
  • 3 When ready to leave, locate a parking teller machine (lobby or festival foyer). Insert your white ticket first, wait for the price, then insert the green pass to zero out the total.
  • 4 Use your zeroed out white pass to exit the parking lot without having to pay.
